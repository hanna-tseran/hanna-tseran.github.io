I am a Ph.D. student in computer science working in Guido Montúfar's group at Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (MPI MiS) in Leipzig, Germany. I am also a student at International Max Planck Research School for Mathematics in the Sciences (IMPRS MiS).

I am broadly interested in machine learning, leaning to the theoretical side. These days I am focusing on deep learning theory and neural network expressivity in particular.
