I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in computer science working in Guido Montúfar's group at Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (MPI MiS) in Leipzig, Germany. I am also a student at International Max Planck Research School for Mathematics in the Sciences (IMPRS MiS). I expect to graduate in ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{July 2023}}}$. 

I am broadly interested in ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{machine learning}}}$, focusing more on the ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{mathematical aspects}}}$. I believe we should comprehend how the existing deep learning systems work to build more advanced ones. Hence, I am particularly fascinated by the ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{theory of deep neural networks}}}$ for its benefits for understanding deep learning mechanics, knowledgeably designing machine learning models, and ensuring positive societal impacts.

I am curious about various topics in the field, but recently I have been incredibly excited by neural network ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{expressivity}}}$, ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{generalization}}}$, and ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{optimization}}}$. Lately, I have been concentrating more on the effects of the ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{activation function}}}$ and ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{network initialization}}}$ on these questions and studying ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{over-parametrization}}}$, ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{NTK}}}$, and ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{implicit bias}}}$. Additionally, I enjoy considering what it would take for the models to operate in the real world. With this intent, I studied ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{few-shot learning}}}$, ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{continual learning}}}$, and ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{long-term memory in conversational AI}}}$. My other interest is practical architectures, such as ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{transformers}}}$, ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{graph neural networks}}}$, and ${\color[RGB]{0,77,77}\underline{\text{Bayesian neural networks}}}$.

_I am currently looking for research positions both in academia and industry. Feel free to contact me at my e-mail: hanna.tseran at gmail.com._
